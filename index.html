Introduction

Dr. Zhiping Cai is a full Professor in Computing Science Department, College of Computer, National University of Defense Technology (NUDT) at Changsha, China. He has visited iQua Lab in 2013, which is leaded by Prof. Baochun Li. Zhiping Cai received his bachelor's, master's and Ph.D degree in computer science and technology with honor from NUDT in July 1996, April 2002 and December 2005, respectively. His doctoral dissertation has been rewarded with the Outstanding Dissertation Award of the Chinese PLA. Zhiping Cai received the Silver Awards for Education from the Chinese PLA in July 2009.
